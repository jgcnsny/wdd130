Philippines

The Philippines is a captivating archipelago in Southeast Asia, renowned for its breathtaking beauty that seamlessly blends nature's wonders with rich cultural diversity. With its tropical climate and over 7,000 islands, the country boasts pristine beaches adorned with powdery white sand and crystal-clear turquoise waters. Palawan, often hailed as the "Last Frontier," hosts the enchanting Underground River, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and the stunning El Nido, known for its dramatic limestone cliffs and hidden lagoons
